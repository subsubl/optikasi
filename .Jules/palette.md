## 2024-10-27 - Focus Management in Auto-advancing Wizards
**Learning:** In Vue, auto-advancing components (like quizzes) destroy the actively focused element (the button that triggered the step change). This drops focus onto the `<body>`, heavily disorienting screen reader and keyboard users.
**Action:** When creating wizard-like auto-advancing components, wrap the dynamic step content in a container with `tabindex="-1"` and `focus:outline-none`. Use a `watch` on the step variable alongside `nextTick()` to programmatically call `.focus({ preventScroll: true })` on this container, ensuring smooth continuation for assistive technologies.
